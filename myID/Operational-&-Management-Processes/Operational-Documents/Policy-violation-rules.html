Policy Violation Rules

 

  1. Non-Privileged users cannot request Privileged Entitlements or Privileged Roles with Privileged Entitlements (Azure and Windows AD apps), for remaining all application privileged/non-privileged entitlements can be requested.
  2. Shared Mailbox accounts can request only Mailing Group Entitlements, other than mailing group entitlements cannot be requested for Shared Mailbox accounts.
  3. Phone Contacts cannot request for any of the entitlements.
  4. Mail Contacts can request only Mailing Group Entitlements, other than mailing group entitlements cannot be requested for Shared Mailbox accounts.
  5. Business Partners can request for Cloud Application (Azure AD) groups only.
  6. Identities with “Test Business Role for One Account” role can have only one account.
  7. Call Queues and Auto Attendants cannot request for any of the entitlements.
